Croc Clash — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

Overview

Croc Clash ("the Game") is a free-to-play browser-based fighting game. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Player Name: A display name you choose, stored locally on your device via localStorage. We do not transmit this to any server.
  • Game Progress: Win/loss records, career stats, and unlocked content are stored locally on your device.
  • TikTok Account Data: When playing as a TikTok Mini Game, we use TikTok's silent login to obtain your OpenID (a unique anonymous identifier). We do not access your TikTok profile, videos, followers, or personal information unless you explicitly authorize it.
  • Online Multiplayer: When using online multiplayer, game state data (positions, actions, HP) is transmitted between players via WebRTC peer connections. No gameplay data is stored on our servers beyond the duration of the match.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not collect location data, biometric data, or device identifiers.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

When running inside TikTok, the game uses TikTok's Mini Games SDK. TikTok's own data practices are governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data. We do not maintain any server-side databases of player information.

Children's Privacy

Croc Clash is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
